About Us

Clinical is an Hospital which is administered and managed in accordance with a Hospital Constitution approved by The Ministry of Health of Romania. The Hospital has a total of 99 beds – 89 In-Patient beds and 10 Day Care beds. The In-Patient specialties are Cardiology, Image Diagnosis, Cardic Surgery. We also have an Urgent Care Centre incorporating a Local Injuries Unit and Medical Assessment Unit.
